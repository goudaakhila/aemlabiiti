Dr. Saptarshi Ghosh

Head Of the Applied Electromagnetic Lab

Dr. Saptarshi Ghosh is an Assistant Professor in the Discipline of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore. With a research focus on electromagnetics, his expertise encompasses frequency selective surfaces, metamaterials, microwave absorbers, and microwave antennas. Alongside his teaching responsibilities, Dr. Ghosh actively contributes to advancing the understanding and application of these areas. His dedication to fostering a stimulating learning environment inspires students to explore the fascinating world of electromagnetics and its diverse applications. Overall, Dr. Saptarshi Ghosh's contributions and passion for research and teaching make him an invaluable asset to the academic community at the Indian Institute of Technology Indore.
